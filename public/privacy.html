🛡️ RentNestle is committed to protecting your privacy. We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve our rental services.
01

Information We Collect

When you use RentNestle, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification: Name, mobile number, email address, and profile photo.
  • Identity Verification: Aadhaar number (last 4 digits only), PAN card details, for KYC verification purposes.
  • Property Information: Address, photos, rent details, and amenity information provided by landlords when listing a property.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to show nearby properties and enable Street View features.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, searches performed, listings viewed, and interactions with the platform.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our platform between tenants and landlords.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details for plan purchases and stamp duty payments (processed securely through Razorpay — we do not store card details).
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your RentNestle account
  • Connect tenants with landlords for rental properties
  • Generate and process digital rental agreements with Aadhaar e-sign and e-stamp
  • Verify tenant and landlord identities for safety
  • Send OTP messages for authentication via SMS or WhatsApp
  • Send automated HRA receipts, agreement notifications, and platform updates
  • Provide AI-powered property descriptions and tenant matching
  • Improve our platform through analytics and usage data
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian law
  • Prevent fraud and maintain platform security
03

WhatsApp & Communication

RentNestle uses the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send you important notifications including:

  • OTP verification messages for login and registration
  • New inquiry alerts for landlords
  • Agreement signing reminders and confirmations
  • Monthly HRA receipt delivery
  • Property visit scheduling confirmations
  • AI-powered responses to your WhatsApp queries in English, Tamil, and Hindi

By registering on RentNestle with your mobile number, you consent to receive these WhatsApp messages. You may opt out at any time by contacting us or replying STOP to any WhatsApp message.

We do not use your WhatsApp number for marketing or advertising purposes without your explicit consent.

04

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your information only with trusted service providers necessary to deliver our services:

  • Supabase — Secure cloud database and authentication
  • MSG91 / Twilio — SMS OTP delivery
  • Meta (WhatsApp) — WhatsApp Business messaging
  • Digio / Leegality — Aadhaar e-sign and digital stamping
  • Razorpay — Secure payment processing
  • Google — Maps, Street View, and Places Autocomplete
  • OpenAI — AI-powered listing descriptions and chatbot
  • Vercel — Website hosting and deployment

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the specified purpose. We never sell your personal data to advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party.

We may disclose your information to government authorities or law enforcement if required by Indian law, court order, or legal process.

05

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Database access is protected with Row Level Security (RLS)
  • Aadhaar and KYC documents are stored in private, encrypted storage buckets
  • API keys and secrets are never exposed to the client
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • OTP-based authentication — no passwords stored

While we take every precaution, no internet transmission is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

06

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and applicable Indian law, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@rentnestle.com. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Cookies & Tracking

RentNestle uses essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your search preferences and filters
  • Store your language preference (English, Tamil, Hindi)
  • Enable Progressive Web App (PWA) functionality for offline use

We use Vercel Analytics for anonymous usage statistics. This data does not identify individual users. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

08

Children's Privacy

RentNestle is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under the age of 18.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@rentnestle.com and we will delete the information promptly.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send a notification via WhatsApp or SMS to registered users
  • Display a notice on the RentNestle homepage

Your continued use of RentNestle after the effective date of the revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
